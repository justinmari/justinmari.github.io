game design | game development

The first spark that sent me spiraling into my interest in game design was learning how to create a simple RPG using RPGMaker (I'm a big fan of Undertale). This led to many early projects of mine, which ranged from Unity tutorials (Top-Down Space Shooter) to spontaneous bouts of wanting to code (Friend-Inspired Sidescroller Game). Using my highschool programming classes along with my earlier projects, I developed a solid foundation in the programming basics.


web design | web development

Front end development hooked me into the world of web development and web design. Once I have discovered the use of JavaScript in combination with the <canvas> element, I realized the vast possibilities when it comes to sleek visuals and animations.


coffee brewing

I was never really a coffee person until I ended up as a part-time coffee barista at a café. Espresso brewing caught my eye the most, which led to me investing in my own espresso setup at home. Though the process of brewing good espresso was a long and difficult journey, I eventually found the balance that best suited my taste. I mainly enjoy drinking specialty espresso drinks, but I get the most joy when making them for others.


boardgames | cardgames | game hosting

If there's any way to develop trust, teamwork, and leadership (I'm looking at you hiring manager), hosting a participating in boardgames and cardgames are an excellent tool. Some games I've really come to love are:

  • Exploding Kittens
  • Betrayal at House on the Hill
  • Codenames
  • Risk

These games really brought my family and friends together, especially during the pandemic where everyone is stuck inside their homes. As much as I love competitive games such as Risk and Exploding Kittens, I enjoy co-operative games more such as Betrayal at House on the Hill.


game server hosting

Videogames are great, but they would be even better when played with friends. Minecraft was the catalyst towards me learning about server hosting and the basics of how ports work on a router. Using this knowledge, I was able to teach myself about the general idea of LANs and WLANs, which have enabled my friends to play together in many games. Some dedicated servers I have hosted were for Minecraft, Garrys Mod, Terraria, and Starbound.
